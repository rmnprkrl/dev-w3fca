Custody Providers

  1. Please contact your preferred custody provider in order to receive your DOT address.
  2. Once you have received your DOT address, proceed to the next slide in order to add your address to the Polkadot Claims App.
  3. Once your address is added to the Claims App, please proceed to Step 3 “Claim & Attest” below.

After you successfully claimed your tokens, your DOT balance will appear in your account with your custody provider.

PLEASE NOTE: You can only claim your tokens to a custodial account if you have not previously claimed them. If you have previously claimed your tokens to a different address, you can only transfer them to your custody provider once transferability has been enabled on the network. Please refer to our launch roadmap for more information.
